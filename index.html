My Journey into the Sport of Boxing

I have a burning passion for Boxing. Boxing is a great sport for young adolescent males with pent-up agression and full of testosterone. I have seen a lot of the classic fights and trilogies from the 70's, 80's and 90's such as: Muhammad Ali vs Joe Frazier III (Thrilla in Manilla), Thomas Hearns vs Marvin Hagler, and Evander Holyfield vs Riddick Bowe.


Truth be told, my favourite weight divison in boxing has to be the heavyweight division. You never know when the fight might end, and the underdog always has a punchers chance of causing an upset. Allow me to spring to your mind the eve of 25th February 1990 when a 42/1 underdog named J James 'Buster' Douglas sent out shockwaves to the boxing world whenever he defeated then Undisputed Heavyweight Champion, 'Iron' Mike Tyson to cause the biggest upset in boxing history.
